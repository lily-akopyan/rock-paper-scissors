How to play

Do you really not know how to play rock paper scissors? *sigh* okay, here we go:

You and your opponent chose a weapon (rock paper or scissors duh) and the battle begins...

  • Rock beats Scissors (because rock breaks the blades)
  • Scissors beat Paper (scissors can cut paper how surprising)
  • Paper beats Rock (paper wraps around the rock and that defeats it somehow??? I dunno just go with it dude)

The winner of the round gets a point, if it's a draw noone gets points.

Play until you or your opponent get 5 points.

And that's it! Enjoy the game you weirdo...
